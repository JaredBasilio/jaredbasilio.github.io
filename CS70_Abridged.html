stable matching

The Stable Matching Algorithm is a pairing of two groups of n such that the resulting pair is happy. Each individual of each group will have a ranking of the other candidates of the other group.
Stable Matching Procedure for a day
Morning All jobs will send an offer to the candidate on the top of their list
Afternoon Each candidate that received an offer will reject all offers except the one that is the highest on their respective list.
Evening Each job that just got rejected will cross off that candidate from the list.
Repeat this process until all candidates and jobs are paired
Observations from this algorithm:
  • Will always halt with a matching taking at most \[n^2\] days.
  • Improvement Lemma: the job on the candidates string can only get better while the candidate on the jobs string can only get worse.
  • Rogue Pairing: There exists a job and candidate that prefers each other over their current matching. This implies that matching is unstable.
  • The group that is offering is optimal for them. i.e Stable Matching is Job Optimal
